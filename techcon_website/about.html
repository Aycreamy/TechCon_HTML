History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small community meetup and has since grown into one of the most influential global tech conferences. Over the years, we've welcomed thousands of developers, engineers, entrepreneurs, and students from around the world.

Mission

Our mission is to foster collaboration, inspire innovation, and elevate technology for social good. We believe in a future where technology is inclusive, sustainable, and empowering for all.
